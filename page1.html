About McLaren

Since entering the sport in 1966 under the guidance of founder Bruce McLaren, the team’s success has been nothing short of breathtaking. Over five decades have yielded countless victories, pole positions, and nine constructors' championships. Many greats raced for McLaren, including Emerson Fittipaldi, Ayrton Senna, Mika Hakkinen, and Lewis Hamilton.

History b/w 2019 and 2024

2024

  • Rise back to the top to clinch first constructors’ championship since 1998, despite Red Bull dominating first half of season.

2023

  • After a slow start, become regular podium contenders, with Lando Norris and impressive rookie team mate Oscar Piastri scoring nine rostrums between them.
  • The result is a rise to fourth overall in the constructors’ championship.

2022

  • Drop to P5 in the standings behind Alpine, with Lando Norris scoring their sole podium at Imola and 122 of their 159 points.
